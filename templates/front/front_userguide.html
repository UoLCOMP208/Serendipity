User Guide

Publish Posts

Click “PUBLISH POST” on the post list page. Name a title and choose a board first. Type something in the box and then submit it.

Search Posts

In the post list page, you can enter keywords in the search bar to find relevant posts.

View Posts

You can enter the post list page and view all the posts by clicking here. After doing so, you can click the 'view' button to see the details of the post.

Sort Posts

In the post list page, you can sort the posts according to the time when they were published, the featured posts or most comments.

Login or Register

Click the “Login” on the navigation bar, fill in the form and login to Serendipity. If you don't have an account, click “Register” at its right.

Management System

Only for super admins. Forum admins can manage user information and post information here.
